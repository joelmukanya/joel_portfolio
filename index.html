About Me

My name is Joel Tshimanga Mukanya, I was born in Durban, Kwazulu Natal but was raised in pretoria gauteng.

I graduated at Tshwane university of technology in 2014 as a Software Developer and I've gain knowledge in data science, database, programming, data analysis, project management and many more.

I am currently employed as a coding professor at Life Choices and I've my own business, JT Devs. I am also part-time YouTuber where I am teaching people different programming languages.
